Mizu Mizu Meat Barbeque

This recipe is one of the sanjis super licking good barbecue that will make your tummy full every time you eat or even just thinking about it this barbecue are seen on the water 7 arc

Ingredients:

  • 500g diced beef
  • 1 “long onion” (scallion, shallot or green onion)
  • ¼ sweet pepper
  • ½ zucchini
  • 50mL water
  • ½ tbsp. salt
  • ½ lemon
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ¼ onion
  • dash of pepper
  • ½ tsp vegetable oil

Recipe Intructions:

  1. Slice (a)'s lemon, garlic and onion. Place all (a) ingredients in a plastic bag with meat and marinate, a minimum of 1 hour
  2. Slice the “long onion” into 3 cm long pieces, pepper into bite size chunks, and zucchini into 1 cm thick circular slices.
  3. Place the meat from step one and vegetables from step two on skewers. Cook until they reach a nice “grilled” color.
