Japanese Marine Curry

Recipe Instructions

  1. Add your soy sauce, sugar, and salt to the dashi (soup stock) and heat over medium heat until it comes to a light simmer.
  2. Begin preparing your toppings by squeezing out the water from the boiled spinach and slicing them into 3 inch pieces.
  3. Cut your fish cakes into 1 inch pieces.
  4. Boil 2 liters of water in a large pot of water.
  5. Also heat another 1 liter of water in a separate pot.
  6. Put soba noodles into 2 liters of hot water and stir well.
  7. Boil according to the directions on the package for your noodles.
  8. Rinse your noodles under hot water after they're boiled and then put them back into the hot water that was heated separately (the 1 liter water).
  9. Drain the water from the soba in a strainer and serve it in a donburi bowl.
  10. Pour soup over noodles and top with spinach, fish cake, and tensaku.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 yellow onions, cut into wedges
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 2 carrots, cut into wedges
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 apple, peeled and grated
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 potatoes, cut into 1 inch chunks
  • 1 packet S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix
